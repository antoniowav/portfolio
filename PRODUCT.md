# PRODUCT.md — Antonio · Personal Creator Hub

register: brand

## Product purpose

A single-page personal hub for Antonio, a solo product builder in Gothenburg. One
link that says who he is and routes people to every product, the templates store,
and his socials. This is the "link in bio" the social strategy depends on. The
design IS the product: it doubles as a portfolio piece. Distinct from the Coda
studio site (Coda = studio / client-facing; this = the builder, first person).

## Users

1. **New social followers** (Threads / TikTok) landing from a bio link. Want the
   story plus a reason to follow or subscribe. Mobile-first, skim in seconds.
2. **Potential clients / collaborators** — routed onward to the Coda studio site.
3. **Template buyers** — routed to the store (Hushållet is the lead product).

## Positioning & voice

Antonio — solo product builder in Gothenburg. Italian roots, fast shipper, strong
taste, building a studio's worth of products one at a time. Warm, confident,
personal. First person, never corporate, not a faceless agency. The narrative is
"watch one person build a whole studio." Momentum and craft are the brand.

## Strategic principles

- One clear primary action per section. Hero CTA: Follow on Threads.
- Products and socials are typed arrays. Adding a product is a one-line edit.
- Building-in-public momentum is a feature: surface a live "currently shipping" line.
- The link gets posted a lot. Per-page OG image and meta must look great when shared.

## Anti-references (what this must NOT look like)

- Generic shadcn / Inter / neutral-gray SaaS template.
- Centered-hero boilerplate with a big-number hero-metric block.
- Terminal / monospace developer-portfolio aesthetic (the old site was this; retired).
- Faceless agency tone. Corporate third person.

## Out of scope (v1)

Blog / CMS, multi-page, analytics dashboards. Ship one beautiful page. A /writing
route can come later.
